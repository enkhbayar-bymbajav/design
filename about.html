  • Content planning

    A great content plan is the most important step in creating an engaging website. ‚≠ê

    Define the purpose of the page:
    Why does this page exist? What problem is it solving? What does it need to communicate? This simple exercise will lay the foundation for the content, tone and imagery on this page.

    Draft your headline & introduction:
    Create an engaging headline and introduction that helps your readers know they're in the right place.

    Write the body:
    Begin adding ideas for the written content that might appear on this page. It doesn't have to be the final copy but it will give everyone a feel for what you're aiming to communicate.

    Sketch the layout:
    Grab a pen and sketch it out. A simple sketch is also a useful reference for the developers who will need to build the website.

    Add images and video:
    Use images from the web or open free built-in image library to quickly find inspiring examples.

    Build on your ideas:
    A content plan is especially helpful for this step as you can easily connect and work together online.

    Repeat this process for each page:
    Take a step back and look at the overall structure of your website by making a Sitemap. This lets you organize the content for your entire website in one place.
